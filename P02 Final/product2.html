Description

Founded in 1983, Air Jordan is a basketball sneaker brand produced by Nike along with NBA superstar Michael Jordan. The Air Jordan series was specfically made for Jordan throughout his career in basketball.

The Air Jordan 11 retro is made from combining two colours of leather together. One of the most iconic moments was when Michael Jordan geared up for the Chicago Bulls. The red and white is closely associated with one of the best franchises in the league. Filter holes are placed within the triangles to give the shoe breathability and comfort.
