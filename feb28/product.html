Description

Founded in 1966, Vans is an American company that specfically manufactured skateboarding shoes. With over 50 years of history Vans has risen in the ranks of the fashion industry, making a brand that can't be ignored.

The Vans U Old Skools uses advanced, modern materials to create a progressive, premium, pioneering brand of sneakers. These shoes feature a leather and suede combination in order to inhance the comfort and functionality of the shoe. Leather soles wrap around the bottom of the sneaker, creating a classic look.
